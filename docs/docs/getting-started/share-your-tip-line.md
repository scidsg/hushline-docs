## Step 1. Make Yourself Discoverable

Opt-in to the public user directory in **Settings > Profile > Public User Directory** so whistleblowers can find your account.

![Settings](https://github.com/scidsg/hushline-screenshots/blob/main/releases/latest/artvandelay/auth-artvandelay-settings-profile-desktop-light-fold.png?raw=true)

## Step 2. Share Your Address Widely

Add a link to your tip line on your website, email signature, and social media. To find your address go to your profile, and copy the URL in the browser's address bar:
